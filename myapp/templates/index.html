Intro

What I am all about.

Hi! I'm Lotte Wijnants,

Nice to meet you, and thank you for visiting. Let me give you a short introduction of me:

After the realization set in that I will not be making it as a famous singer (no, I wasn't even close..), I started to analyze why I never got to that point. So it is no surprise that I now work everyday to analyze how systems work and how we can optimize processes.

I've always had a second nature to search for new and better ways to become more efficient. I love to strive for a collective goal and will always keep my eyes out for new, creative solutions. As Data Science is one of the fastest growing industries, I truly believe in sharing knowledge and using personal skills.

Ultimately I love puzzling and searching for the best, most effective way. Also, I'm a visual person and like to keep things out of the "black box".
